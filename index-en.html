About

Trusted technical support for Moroccan agriculture

AIT MELLAL LHOUSSAIN provides practical agricultural consulting rooted in real field conditions. The practice covers crop production, irrigation systems, plant nutrition, crop protection, orchard establishment, livestock management and agricultural investment guidance.

Mission

Deliver clear, practical and profitable technical consulting that helps agricultural operators make better decisions on the ground.

Vision

Become a trusted reference for modern, sustainable and results-driven agricultural consulting in Morocco.
